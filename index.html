Welcome to Rugged Tree Woodworking

Legacy: Through the Generations

We believe when we pass our posessions to the next generation they should carry family history and stories with them. Once heirlooms enter the family, generations get to enjoy and appreciate them. With that belief, we strive for durability and craft with care.

Sentimental: The value that matters

Personalized gifts show extra effort was put in to get them the perfect present. Not just something you grabbed at the store the day before. They'll cherish it for years to come. And let's face it, it's hard to sell something with a name on it at a garage sale!

Variety: Tell us what you want

Let us know what you need and we will work with you to ensure a design that is durable, funtional, and fits the style you're after. From picture frames and ornaments to cutting boards and dressers. Even door hangers and welcome signs to coasters and keychains.
