Project Description

A set of snowflake ornaments cut out of 1/8th inch aspen. This set used six of the available designs. Most of them were designed by Isaiah, with a couple designed by his siblings. Each is about three and a half inches in diameter and has a ribbon for hanging. You or your kids can cut out snowflakes and send them in to have them made into wooden snowflakes to get a personalized one of a kind set!
